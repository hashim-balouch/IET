How can I join IET?
You can join IET by signing up for membership in our Office in Electrical Engineering Department GCU.

What is the origin of IET?
Institute of Engineering and Technology is British based society for engineers. This society focuses on the research and enhance the capability of the engineers.

How much is the membership fee?
The membership fee is only 100 rupee.

Does IET On Campus Society have an office?
Yes the office of IET is located in Electrical Engineering Department GCU Lahore.

What are the benefits of being a member of the society?
Gaining valuable experience in professional and personal growth. Participating in various career building events and seminars.

Popular events of IET On Campus?
